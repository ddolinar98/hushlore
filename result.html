Your questions answered

Yes — that's the whole point. Every story you're shown is matched to your quiz answers (mood, voice preference, heat level, tropes). You can retake the quiz anytime, and the more you listen, the better your recommendations get.

Can I listen anytime?
Yes — your library is yours 24/7, on phone, laptop, headphones, anywhere. Stories range from 10 minutes to 40+ minutes, so you can fit a session into any moment.
Are my preferences and data safe?
Your data is private by design. Your bank statement shows a neutral descriptor (no story or genre names). We never share or sell your information. You can delete your account and data anytime.
Is Hushlore for singles only?
Not at all. Listeners use Hushlore solo, in relationships, partnered, single — there's no judgment about how or when. Some people use it alone as a self-care ritual; others share matches with a partner.
